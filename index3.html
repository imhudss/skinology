How to find out your Skin Type?

Determining your skin type is a crucial step in developing an effective skincare routine. One simple method involves washing your face with a mild cleanser and observing how your skin behaves over 30 minutes.

Steps to Find Your Skin Type:

  1. Cleanse Your Face: Start by washing your face with a gentle, mild cleanser to remove any impurities and existing skincare products.
  2. Pat Dry: Gently pat your face dry with a clean, soft towel. Avoid rubbing, as it can irritate the skin.
  3. Wait for 30 Minutes: Allow your skin to air dry naturally without applying any products. During this time, your skin will return to its natural state.
  4. Observe Your Skin: After 30 minutes, observe how your skin feels and looks. Pay attention to the following aspects:
    • Tightness: If your skin feels tight, it may indicate dry skin.
    • Shininess: Excessive shine could suggest oily skin.
    • Balanced: If your skin feels comfortable without tightness or excessive shine, you likely have normal or combination skin.
    • Redness/Irritation: Sensitive skin may show signs of redness or irritation.
  5. Identify Your Skin Type: Based on your observations, categorize your skin type as dry, oily, normal, combination, or sensitive. Understanding your skin type helps you select appropriate skincare products tailored to your specific needs.
